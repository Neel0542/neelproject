Corporate Information - Remington Typewriter Company

The Remington Typewriter Company, founded in the late 1873, played a pivotal role in revolutionizing written communication.

Company History

Established in 1873 by Christopher Latham Sholes, Carlos Glidden, and Samuel W. Soule, the Remington Typewriter Company is credited with manufacturing the iconic Remington No. 1, the world's first commercially successful typewriter.

Mission and Vision

The company's mission was to create innovative writing machines that would enhance efficiency and transform the way people communicated. Their vision was to make writing accessible and convenient for everyone, regardless of profession or location.

Legacy

Over the years, the Remington Typewriter Company continued to innovate, introducing various models of typewriters, and leaving an indelible mark on the history of written communication.
